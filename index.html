The New Publisher
Playbook

1. Own the relationship

Direct channels beat rented platforms. Build newsletters, apps, communities. If AI is the middleman, go around it.

2. Be the source, not the summary

Break news. Publish original research. Take bold positions. Create what AI has to cite, not what it can paraphrase.

3. Monetize trust, not attention

Subscriptions beat ads when discovery is controlled by algorithms. Charge for expertise, access, and community.

4. Distribution ≠ Search anymore

Your audience lives in WhatsApp groups, Discord servers, niche Substacks. Meet them where they actually are.
